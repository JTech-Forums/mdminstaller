How to Use JTech MDM Installer

Step 1: Open Settings

  • Turn on your device and open the Settings app.

Step 2: About Phone

  • Scroll down and tap About phone (or About device).

Step 3: Become a Developer

  • Find Build number and tap it 7 times quickly until you see the confirmation.

Step 4: Developer Options

  • Go back and open the new Developer options menu.

Step 5: Enable USB Debugging

  • Scroll to USB debugging and switch it On.

Step 6: Connect to PC

  • Plug your device into your PC via USB, then click Connect on this website.

Step 7: Pick Your Device

  • In Chrome or Edge, select your device from the prompt and click Connect.

Step 8: Trust This Computer

  • On your phone, check Always allow from this computer, then tap OK.

Step 9: You’re Connected

  • Bingo! You’re connected — now you can install one of our filters.

Step 10: Join the Community

  • When you’re done (success or error), join JTech Forums — we’d love your feedback!
Note: This tool requires Chrome or Edge browser with WebUSB support.

About JTech MDM Installer

JTech MDM Installer is a web-based tool for deploying Mobile Device Management solutions to Android devices directly from your browser.

Features

  • Direct USB connection to Android devices
  • Pre-configured MDM application profiles
  • Batch APK installation support
  • Real-time installation progress tracking
  • No software installation required

Requirements

  • Chrome or Edge browser (version 61+)
  • Android device with USB debugging enabled
  • USB cable for device connection
